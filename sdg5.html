About SDG 5: Gender Equality

~ SDG 5 aims to achieve gender equality and empower all women and girls.

~ Many females face many challenges just because they are female.

  • Gender-Based Violence
  • Gender Biasness
  • Lack of Proper Education
  • Racial Injustice
  • Cultural Norms
  • Unequal Pay
  • Inadequate Healthcare

In short, many females are not granted the rights that males have. Whether thay live in places that are aware or not aware of the struggles.

Which is why this goal was made. By empowering all females of all ages to rise to the occasion and raise awareness of all their struggles and propose to change the way their regular norms; more people will rise and fight to helpwomen achieve equal rights, thus promoting equality among both genders.
